Tips & Limitations

Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting and avoid glare or reflections
  • Crop close to the damaged area when possible
  • Capture straight-on angles for better clarity
  • Ensure the image is clear and in focus
  • Avoid motion blur or low-resolution images

Limitations

  • Scratches and dents can be visually similar
  • Low confidence (<60%) requires manual review
  • Multiple damage types in one image may confuse the model
  • Subtle damage may be missed in low-contrast images
  • Model trained on specific dataset characteristics
